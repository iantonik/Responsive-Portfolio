About Me

Iwona Antonik is an Agile Coach and Trainer working at enterprise and team levels. Iwona has worked in the IT industry for 19 years, gaining valuable experience in the end-to-end product develop process, while holding various positions ranging from technical support to project management to Agile coaching.

Over the last 8 years, Iwona has been providing Agile-focused training and coaching to companies spanning from late-stage startups to large corporations. She splits her time coaching executives, managers and cross-functional teams, as well as building up Scrum Masters and Product Owners.

In 2006, Iwona Antonik received a Bachelor of Science from the University of Phoenix, majoring in Business Management. She later went on to receive a Master of Business Administration in 2007. Additionally, she holds number of industry certifications: Certified Scrum Master (CSM), Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), SAFe Program Consultant (SPC), Professional Scrum Master (PSM), and Project Management Professional (PMP).

Iwona believes that functions within an Agile team should not be isolated silos, and that all functions benefit from as much cross-pollination as possible. She has been practicing what she preaches: Iwona has successfully completed a number of courses from highly regarded universities on varied topics involved in Software Development, ranging from engineering to business applications of Artificial Intelligence and Design Thinking.
